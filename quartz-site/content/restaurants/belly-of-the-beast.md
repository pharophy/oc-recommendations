## Description

Belly of the Beast is the tiny speakeasy hidden inside Stowaway, entered through a giant whale-mouth facade and run as a separate, more intimate cocktail room with its own identity.

## What Makes It Unique

The entrance gimmick is not the whole story, but it is a strong one. Few OC bars lean this hard into a themed secret-room reveal, and the small scale makes it feel closer to a mini attraction than a standard back-bar extension.

## Notes

- Reservations: The official page offers reservations and a waitlist, and the room appears to be very small.
- Dress code: Casual.
- Age policy: I did not find a clean official age statement on the current page, so I marked it `Mixed / Time-dependent`. In practice it reads as a late-night cocktail speakeasy more than a family stop.
- Other: Best used as the second round of a Stowaway visit rather than a stand-alone dinner destination.
