## Description

Pan Am Experience recreates a glamour-era first-class international flight inside Air Hollywood's aviation sets, with vintage-style service, check-in theatrics, and a multi-course meal built around the fantasy of 1970s air travel.

## What Makes It Unique

This is one of the most transportive dining concepts in Southern California because the entire premise depends on role-play, set design, and nostalgia rather than just themed decor around a normal meal.
