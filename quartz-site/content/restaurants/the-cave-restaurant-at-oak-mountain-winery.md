## Description

The Cave Restaurant is Temecula's most literal atmosphere play: you are dining inside the winery's mined underground cave rather than in a normal tasting-room dining area.

## What Makes It Unique

Unlike themed bars that simulate transport, this one relies on an actual subterranean environment. Stone walls, cave lighting, and winery context make it destination-worthy even before you factor in food or wine.

## Notes

- Reservations: Oak Mountain says reservations are not mandatory, but recommends them for weekends and groups of six or more.
- Dress code: Casual wine-country dining.
- Age policy: The dining page includes a `Kids Menu`, which is the clearest signal that children are welcome.
- Other: Best paired with a cave tour, tasting, or full winery afternoon rather than treated as a stand-alone city restaurant.
