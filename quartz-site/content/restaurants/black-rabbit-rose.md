## Description

Black Rabbit Rose is a compact Hollywood hideaway that mixes craft cocktails, late-night bites, roaming close-up magic, and ticketed theater shows in a velvet-heavy lounge setting.

## What Makes It Unique

It lands between speakeasy, cabaret room, and magic club rather than reading like a normal cocktail bar. The immersive value comes from the room mood, the live performance layer, and the feeling that dinner or drinks can turn into a staged show at any point.

## Notes

- Reservations: The official site links to dinner and music reservations plus separate magic-show tickets. Recent Yelp business details describe the dining room as walk-in first-come, first-served, with theater tickets sold separately.
- Dress code: The official site includes a dress-code page, but the rule text was not surfaced cleanly in this refresh. Treat it as a dress-up date-night spot rather than casual daytime dining.
- Age policy: I did not find a clean current official children policy. Because it is a late-night cocktail venue with showtimes and lounge service, I marked it `Mixed / Time-dependent` rather than guessing.
