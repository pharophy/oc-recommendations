## Description

The Cellar is less theatrical than Strong Water or Medieval Times, but it absolutely qualifies as a destination room. It is a basement fine-dining restaurant built around an old-world, candlelit atmosphere that people choose specifically for the mood.

## What Makes It Unique

The strongest hook is the setting itself. The restaurant trades on a subterranean, European-feeling dining room under Villa del Sol, and that physical environment gives it a more transportive identity than most Orange County special-occasion restaurants.

## Notes
