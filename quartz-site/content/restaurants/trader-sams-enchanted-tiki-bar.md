## Description

Trader Sam's is Disneyland's enchanted tiki bar, built around Jungle Cruise-style artifacts, interactive drinks, dense theming, and a room designed to feel like a mischievous tropical outpost.

## What Makes It Unique

It is one of Southern California's most polished themed dining spaces. The room, menu language, lighting, and sound cues all support the same playful adventure-world fantasy.

## Notes

- Reservations: Recommended through Disneyland; same-day options can be limited.
- Dress code: Casual.
- Age policy: Disneyland says all ages are welcome in the enclosed area until 8:00 p.m.; after 8:00 p.m., the enclosed area is 21+ only.
- Other: As of March 23, 2026, Disneyland says the indoor dining room is under renovation through spring 2026, while the outdoor area remains available.
