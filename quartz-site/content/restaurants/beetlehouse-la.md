## Description

BeetleHouse LA is a year-round Halloween restaurant and show space with a Burtonesque horror-comedy mood, themed cocktails, dark decor, and a fixed-price dinner-show format.

## What Makes It Unique

This is one of the clearest "fully committed concept restaurant" entries in Los Angeles. The entire experience is built around gothic camp, character performance, spooky visuals, and audience-facing spectacle rather than just menu theming.

## Notes

- Reservations: Recommended, though the site says walk-ins may be possible.
- Dress code: Costumes and theme-friendly looks fit the room.
- Age policy: Official site says children under 12 are allowed for dinner pricing, but bars and patio are 21+ and reservations after 9:30 p.m. are 21+.
- Other: The site currently lists a three-course dinner-show experience at $65 for guests 12 and older.
