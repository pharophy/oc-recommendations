## Description

The Cauldron is a fantasy-coded cocktail and casual-food stop built around potion language, witchy branding, and a playful immersive frame.

## What Makes It Unique

This one works because it does not hedge. The concept is obvious, the drinks support it, and the room feels designed for a themed night out instead of a standard sports-bar layout with decorations.
