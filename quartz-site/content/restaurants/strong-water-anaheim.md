## Description

Strong Water is a narrative tiki destination with a shipwreck-backstory setting, dense decor, and cocktails that feel designed for the room rather than generic tropical service.

## What Makes It Unique

It feels like stepping into a themed environment, not just a bar with bamboo. The world-building, soundtrack, and drinks all push the same adventure-fantasy mood.

## Notes

- Reservations: Required for indoor seating.
- Dress code: Casual.
- Age policy: The venue states indoor dining is 21+ only; other seating rules can vary, so treat this as time- and location-dependent.
- Other: Excellent fit for a Disney-adjacent or tiki-focused night.
