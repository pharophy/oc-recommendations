## Description

Momoku No Usagi is an upstairs Japanese speakeasy in San Clemente with a whiskey-led cocktail program, sushi/small plates, and a rule-heavy "speak easy" attitude that makes the room feel deliberately separate from the street outside.

## What Makes It Unique

The immersion here comes from control and intimacy rather than giant scenery. The house rules, upstairs entrance, small reservations-only feel, and focused cocktail program give it a proper hidden-room energy instead of a standard sushi-bar setup.

## Notes

- Reservations: The official site emphasizes reserved seats and a 90-minute experience window.
- Dress code: The official rules explicitly say not to "dress like a hobo" and require shoes; interpret that as beach-town casual with some effort.
- Age policy: The official site says `We are a Twenty-One and Over Establishment.`
- Other: Stronger fit for cocktails and atmosphere than for a full multi-course dinner.
