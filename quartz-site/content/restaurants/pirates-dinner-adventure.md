## Description

Pirates Dinner Adventure is full-scale family spectacle: a pirate ship, indoor lagoon, aerial work, sword fights, pyrotechnics, and a meal built around cheering sections and audience participation.

## What Makes It Unique

The room itself is the hook. Few Southern California dining-adjacent experiences go this hard on physical set-building, stunt performance, and "you are in the show now" energy.

## Notes

- Reservations: Official site pushes advance ticket purchases and reserved seating packages.
- Dress code: Casual.
- Age policy: The official site describes the show as `family-friendly`, and the ticketing/menu pages separate adult, child, and infant pricing.
- Other: Better for spectacle, birthdays, and groups than for food-first dining.
