## Description

The Obscure is a ticketed cocktail-and-storytelling experience built around house-distilled spirits, guided pours, live art, and a candlelit fantasy environment.

## What Makes It Unique

This is one of the strongest non-restaurant immersive drink experiences in Los Angeles. The experience is structured like a narrative tasting rather than an ordinary seated bar visit.

## Notes

- Reservations: Advance ticket purchase is required and sellouts appear common.
- Dress code: All-black attire is welcomed but not required.
- Age policy: The official FAQ says invitees must be of legal drinking age.
- Other: The current format is a six-drink guided tasting running about 90 to 120 minutes.
