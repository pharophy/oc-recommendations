## Description

Raised by Wolves is a hidden cocktail bar concealed behind a rotating fireplace inside a bottle shop at Westfield UTC.

## What Makes It Unique

The entrance reveal is the hook, but the interior is what keeps it in the list: ornate old-world styling, high-concept cocktail design, and a room that feels wildly out of place in the middle of a mall.

## Notes

- Reservations: Recommended for the bar; the official site lists current bar hours separately from the retail shop.
- Dress code: Smart casual fits the space best.
- Age policy: I did not find a fully explicit official rule for children in the bar, so treat this as adults-focused and confirm before visiting.
- Other: Third-party references consistently describe the fireplace entrance and elaborate decor, including 50 Best Discovery and Hidden San Diego.
