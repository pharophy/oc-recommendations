# Oak Canyon Nature Nights 2026

Nature Nights is one of the better developmental event series in the area because it combines novelty, real-world science topics, and the atmosphere of being out in a canyon after hours.

## Why It Stands Out

This turns a normal nature-center visit into a recurring special outing. It feels more memorable than a daytime docent walk because the timing and format add a real event feel.

## 2026 Timing

- Runs every Wednesday from June 24 through August 5, 2026.

## Practical Notes

- Each evening begins with a twilight walk at 7:00 p.m. followed by a 7:30 p.m. presentation.
- Anaheim lists weekly themes including reptiles, birds of prey, wild wonders, fire and ice, the solar system, and a flashlight walk.
- Admission is free, registration is required, and a $5 donation per family is suggested.
