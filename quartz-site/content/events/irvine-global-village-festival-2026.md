# Irvine Global Village Festival 2026

Irvine Global Village Festival is one of the stronger large-scale culture festivals in Orange County when you want broad cultural variety, family activities, and low planning friction.

## Why It Stands Out

Compared with smaller single-culture events, this one is broader and easier for sampling. Compared with many huge regional festivals, parking and family logistics are usually simpler.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for Saturday, October 10, 2026 from 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- The City of Irvine highlights food, music, art, cultural exhibits, kids' activities, and a global marketplace.
- Free parking is available, with premier parking offered separately.
