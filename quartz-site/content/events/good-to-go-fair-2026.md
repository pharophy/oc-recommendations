# Good To Go Fair 2026

Pretend City's Good To Go Fair is more useful than a generic family expo because it concentrates early-childhood resources, screenings, literacy, STEM, and local organizations into one practical event with a clear audience.

## Why It Stands Out

This is not just entertainment. It has a stronger function for families with younger children, which makes it a distinct kind of Orange County event compared with standard weekend festivals.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for April 18, 2026 from 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- The fair is held in Pretend City's parking lot.
- The event focuses on healthy development, literacy, STEM, screenings, and community resources.
- Best fit is families with younger children.
