# Jr. Ranger Adventure at Clark Regional Park on April 11, 2026

This is one of the better April outings if you want structured outdoor learning tied to the same fossil and geology interests that already resonate with your child.

## Why It Stands Out

It is more engaging than a self-guided park visit because the activity has a built-in mission, a badge, and a guided context.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for Saturday, April 11, 2026 from 3:00 p.m. to 4:30 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- OC Parks says participants meet in front of the Interpretive Center and take a half-mile walk through geologic time.
- Designed for ages 7 to 12, but all are welcome.
- Registration is required and parking is $5 per vehicle.
