# Dana Point Festival of Whales 2026

Dana Point's Festival of Whales is one of the most distinctive annual events in Orange County because the setting, harbor energy, and whale-season context all reinforce the theme instead of treating it as a generic street fair.

## Why It Stands Out

It has the kind of built-in sense of place that many festivals do not. Even without every add-on event, the harbor setting gives the weekend a stronger coastal identity than most family festivals in the region.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for March 6 to March 8, 2026.

## Practical Notes

- The official site highlights trolley and shuttle options.
- Some activities are free while others are separately ticketed.
- This works best if you treat it as a harbor-area day rather than just a quick stop.
