# Laguna Beach First Thursdays Art Walk

Laguna's First Thursdays Art Walk is one of the best recurring culture nights in the county. It pairs walkable gallery-hopping with a more beautiful setting than most downtown events.

## Why It Stands Out

This is a strong step up if you want local event energy but with more art and atmosphere. It feels like a real Laguna tradition rather than a one-off city activation.

## Current Timing

- Held on the first Thursday of every month from 6:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- Visit Laguna Beach lists free trolley service during the event.
- Laguna Art Museum is open complimentary during Art Walk hours.
- Best fit is an early-evening cultural outing rather than a large festival.
