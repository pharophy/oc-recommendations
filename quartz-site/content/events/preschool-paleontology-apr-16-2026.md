# Preschool Paleontology on April 16, 2026

This is one of the best exact-match events for your child's fossil and rock interests. It is small, specific, and tied directly to the Clark Interpretive Center's paleontology focus.

## Why It Stands Out

The program is more aligned with observation, science interest, and hands-on learning than a generic storytime or craft class.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for Thursday, April 16, 2026 from 10:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.

## Practical Notes

- OC Parks says the program includes a visit to the Interpretive Center, a story, a short walk, and a geology-themed craft.
- Designed for ages 2 to 6, though all are welcome.
- Advance registration is required.
- Parking is $3 per vehicle.
