# Sawdust Art Festival 2026

Sawdust Art Festival is one of the strongest Orange County summer culture events because the setting itself is part of the appeal. It feels more like entering a handcrafted village than attending a generic art fair.

## Why It Stands Out

If you like events that feel rooted in a place instead of interchangeable, Sawdust is one of the best fits in the region. It is much more immersive than a normal booth-lined street fair.

## 2026 Timing

- Official Sawdust materials describe the summer festival as running all summer, late June through late August.
- The organization notes that Summer 2026 artist applications are closed.

## Practical Notes

- The festival grounds are in Laguna Canyon at 935 Laguna Canyon Road.
- Sawdust also runs art classes and workshops throughout the year, which can make this a repeat-visit venue rather than a one-time festival.
