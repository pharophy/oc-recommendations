# DTSA First Saturday ArtWalk

DTSA First Saturday ArtWalk is one of the best "fun and local" recurring events in Orange County. It turns downtown Santa Ana into a walkable community arts night with galleries, vendors, music, and a lot more texture than a generic city calendar event.

## Why It Stands Out

This works well if you liked the easy energy of downtown Tustin events but want something denser and more creative. It feels rooted in a real arts district rather than a pop-up event dropped into a parking lot.

## Current Timing

- Held every first Saturday of the month from 5:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- Free and all ages.
- Strongest fit is a flexible evening where wandering is part of the fun.
- Parking strategy matters more here than at a suburban festival.
