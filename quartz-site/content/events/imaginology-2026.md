# Imaginology 2026

Imaginology is one of the best child-development event picks in Orange County. It is built around hands-on STEAM activities, competitions, exhibits, and make-style learning instead of passive entertainment.

## Why It Stands Out

This is one of the clearest examples of a special event that supports curiosity and building skills while still feeling fun. It fits your "development through discovery" criterion better than a typical family fair.
