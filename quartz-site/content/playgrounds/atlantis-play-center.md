# Atlantis Play Center

Atlantis Play Center is one of the clearest next playground picks if you liked places like Dinosaur Park and Adventure Playground. It has a weirder, more memorable identity than a modern generic park.

## Why It Stands Out

The themed structures feel specific and a little eccentric in a good way. It has enough visual personality that the visit feels like a destination, not just a nearby playground.

## Practical Notes

- Garden Grove lists concrete animals, a sea serpent slide, swings, climbers, picnic areas, and seasonal splash-pad water play.
- Admission is $2 for ages 3 and up, and free for ages 2 and under.
- Adults are not allowed without kids, and kids are not allowed without adults.
- Hours vary by season, so check the city page before going.
