# Great Park Balloon & Carousel

Great Park Balloon turns a simple park visit into something much more memorable: a 400-foot tethered ascent with broad views over Irvine and central Orange County, followed by a carousel and the rest of the Great Park grounds.

## Why It Stands Out

Unlike most family attractions in Orange County, this one gives you a genuine aerial-view moment without needing a helicopter or a full day trip. It works well as a low-effort date add-on, a family outing, or a stop before exploring the rest of the park.

## Practical Notes

- Balloon rides are weather-dependent and can pause for wind or conditions.
- Parking at the Great Park is free.
- The balloon is a paid attraction; the carousel is typically free.
- This is easiest to pair with a larger Great Park day rather than treating it as a standalone destination.
