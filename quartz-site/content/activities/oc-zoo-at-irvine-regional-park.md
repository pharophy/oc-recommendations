# OC Zoo at Irvine Regional Park

The OC Zoo is small by design, which is exactly why it works so well. It focuses on plants and animals native to the southwest and becomes much more compelling when treated as one piece of a larger Irvine Regional Park day.

## Why It Stands Out

This is not a destination zoo in the theme-park sense. Its appeal is that it is affordable, low-stress, and unusually easy to combine with the park's train rides, paddle boats, and walking areas for a full family outing.

## Practical Notes

- Admission is $2 for guests ages 3 and up.
- The zoo is inside Irvine Regional Park, so plan around park access and parking.
- Best for families with younger kids or anyone who wants a short, easy animal stop.
