# Tanaka Farms Weekend Wagon Ride

Tanaka Farms is one of the few Orange County outings that can still feel rural, and its weekend breakfast-and-wagon-ride setup gives families or visitors a defined morning plan instead of a vague farm visit.

## Why It Stands Out

The combination of breakfast, barnyard time, and scheduled wagon rides creates a fuller experience than a standard produce stand or petting-zoo stop. It is especially good when you want something outdoorsy but not strenuous.

## Practical Notes

- Weekend breakfast service starts at 7:30 a.m.
- Barnyard access starts at 8:00 a.m.
- Wagon rides run until 9:45 a.m. on weekends.
- Admission is free with breakfast purchase for this offering.
