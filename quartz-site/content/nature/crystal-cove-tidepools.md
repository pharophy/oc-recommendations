# Crystal Cove Tidepools

Crystal Cove is one of the strongest nature outings in Orange County when timed correctly. At low tide, its rocky shoreline becomes a tidepool destination with visible marine life, textured reef areas, and a much more exploratory feel than a standard beach walk.

## Why It Stands Out

This is one of the rare Orange County outings where timing changes the entire experience. On the right day, it becomes a genuine tidepool adventure with a strong discovery element for kids and adults.

## Practical Notes

- Crystal Cove identifies four main tidepool viewing areas: Reef Point, Rocky Bight, Pelican Point, and Treasure Cove.
- The park recommends visiting when tide levels are 1.5 feet or lower, ideally at a negative tide.
- Day-use parking fees apply.
- Removing marine life or natural materials is prohibited.
