# OC Fair 2026

The OC Fair is the most obvious large-format event on this list, but it still belongs here because it is one of the county's clearest seasonal anchors and can deliver a full-day outing with rides, food, exhibits, and concerts.

## Why It Stands Out

Its scale matters. Even for locals, the fair functions more like a seasonal ritual than a routine event, especially because the official run is limited and ticketing is controlled.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for July 17 to August 16, 2026.
- Open Wednesday through Sunday.

## Practical Notes

- Advance ticket purchase is required.
- On-site parking is paid.
- Best for visitors who want a full, high-energy outing rather than a short stop.
