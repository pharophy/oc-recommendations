# Bowers Pacific Islands Festival 2026

Bowers' Pacific Islands Festival belongs on the list if you want recurring, culture-forward museum events that stay family-friendly and easy to do.

## Why It Stands Out

This is part of the museum's stronger recurring festival programming and works well as a medium-sized cultural outing rather than an overwhelming one.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for Sunday, August 2, 2026 from 11:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- Bowers describes it as a family festival in the Key Courtyard.
- Strong fit for families who want performances and simple hands-on activities in a contained setting.
