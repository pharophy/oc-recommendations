# Slow Art Day at Bowers 2026

Slow Art Day is a quieter option than the big family festivals, but it still qualifies as a special event because it changes the way you experience the museum.

## Why It Stands Out

It is one of the better low-intensity cultural outings if you want something thoughtful instead of loud or crowded.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for Saturday, April 11, 2026 from 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- Bowers lists spotlight tours at 11 a.m., 12 p.m., 1 p.m., and 2 p.m.
- Included with general admission.
- Better fit for art-looking than for hands-on kid play.
