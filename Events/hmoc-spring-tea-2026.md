# HMOC Spring Tea 2026

HMOC's spring tea is a more niche event, but it belongs in the collection because it uses the museum grounds and a strong theme to create something with real local personality.

## Why It Stands Out

This is a good example of a small special event that feels intentional rather than generic. The historic setting does a lot of work for the atmosphere.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for Saturday, April 11, 2026 from 11:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- The 2026 theme is "Tea at Tiffany's."
- Gates open at 11:00 a.m. and tea seating starts at 11:30 a.m.
- Better fit for adults or older kids than for a toddler-centered outing.
