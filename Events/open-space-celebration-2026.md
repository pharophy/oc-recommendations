# Open Space Celebration 2026

Irvine's Open Space Celebration stands out because it wraps a guided nature outing, free breakfast, and seasonal community programming into one event instead of asking visitors to self-organize the experience.

## Why It Stands Out

For families or casual hikers, this is a strong entry point into Irvine's open-space system. It feels more approachable than simply telling people to go find a trail on their own.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for April 18, 2026.

## Practical Notes

- Hosted at Quail Hill Trailhead.
- The city lists a free pancake breakfast.
- Guided hikes cover the 1.8-mile Quail Hill Loop.
- Advance registration is required and space is limited.
