# OCBC Hanamatsuri Festival 2026

If you like OC Japan Fair because of the mix of cultural food, browsing, and live atmosphere, this is a strong smaller-scale counterpart. It feels more community-rooted and less commercial, while still offering enough food and cultural density to make the outing feel substantial.

## Why It Stands Out

The Orange County Buddhist Church frames this as a public flower festival celebrating the Buddha's birth, but the draw for most visitors is the broader cultural mix: food, exhibits, demonstrations, games, and live performances across a full weekend window.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for April 18-19, 2026 from 1:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- The official festival page says admission is free and open to the public.
- The church highlights delicious food, cultural exhibits and demonstrations, and games.
- This is a better fit than a giant fair if you want Japanese cultural immersion in a more local, community-led setting.
