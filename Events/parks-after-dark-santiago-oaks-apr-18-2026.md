# Parks After Dark at Santiago Oaks on April 18, 2026

Parks After Dark is one of the better "special local outing" events because the nighttime format changes the whole feel of a familiar park.

## Why It Stands Out

This is a strong fit if you like outings that feel a little adventurous without needing a huge drive or a major ticketed production.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for Saturday, April 18, 2026 from 7:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- OC Parks describes the program as a search for nighttime critters, including toads and owls.
- Warm clothes, sturdy shoes, water, and red-tinted flashlights are recommended.
- Not recommended for very small children or strollers.
- Registration is required and parking is $5 per vehicle.
