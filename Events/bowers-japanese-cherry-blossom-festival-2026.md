# Bowers Japanese Cherry Blossom Festival 2026

This is one of the best smaller-scale cultural immersion events in Orange County if you want something more manageable than a major fair but still rooted in real performances, food-adjacent treats, and hands-on programming.

## Why It Stands Out

It works especially well for families because the art projects, music, and performances are all in one museum courtyard setting instead of spread across a giant fairground.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for Sunday, April 19, 2026 from 11:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- Bowers lists traditional music and dance, taiko drumming, art projects, face painting, a cherry pie treat, and bonsai demonstrations.
- Good fit if you want a cultural event without committing to a full-day festival.
