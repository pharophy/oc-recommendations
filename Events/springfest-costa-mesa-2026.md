# SpringFest Costa Mesa 2026

Costa Mesa's SpringFest is a stronger-than-average local spring event because it is clearly themed, family-oriented, and scheduled as a concentrated daytime outing rather than a vague all-season promotion.

## Why It Stands Out

The 2026 pirate theme gives it more personality than a standard egg-hunt event. It is the kind of family festival that can justify a dedicated morning outing instead of just being an add-on if you happen to be nearby.

## 2026 Timing

- Scheduled for April 4, 2026 from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

## Practical Notes

- The 2026 theme is "A Pirate Adventure."
- The city lists games, treasure egg hunts, train rides, and entertainment.
- The event is at the Lions Park Event Lawn, 1845 Park Avenue.
