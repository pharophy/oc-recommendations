# LOCA at the Library in April 2026

LOCA at the Library is a useful lower-key option when you want a creative family outing without the scale or cost of a festival. It is especially useful as an easy add-on or spontaneous plan.

## Why It Stands Out

This works because the learning is built into the activity, not bolted on. It is art-forward, accessible, and easy to repeat.

## 2026 Timing

- Visit Laguna Beach lists April 11, April 13, April 18, and April 20, 2026 as upcoming April dates.

## Practical Notes

- Workshops are held at Laguna Beach Public Library.
- The listing says workshops for kids ages 5 and up are Mondays at 4:30 p.m., and family workshops together are second Saturdays at 2:00 p.m.
- Free, no registration required, seating first come first served.
