## Description

The Blind Rabbit is a reservation-driven speakeasy inside the Anaheim Packing District, entered through sake barrels and exited through a bookcase.

## What Makes It Unique

The hidden entrance, intimate 30-seat footprint, prohibition styling, and rotating themed pop-ups make this one of Orange County's strongest immersive drinking experiences.

## Notes

- Reservations: Strongly recommended; the official site emphasizes limited seating.
- Dress code: No current formal dress code is being enforced.
- Age policy: Official site says guests must be 21+ and that everyone is carded.
- Other: Corner's January 27, 2026 update specifically mentions storm-cloud ceiling effects and changing pop-up themes.
