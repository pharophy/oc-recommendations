## Description

Jojo's Hideaway is an upscale lounge and supper club hidden within 17th Street Grill in Tustin. The venue explicitly pitches dinner, wine, cocktails, and close-up live entertainment as a single combined night-out experience.

## What Makes It Unique

This is more intimate than theatrical. Its strongest differentiator is the supper-club structure: hidden location, seated lounge feel, and a recurring live program built around jazz, classic rock, blues, and comedy rather than just background music.
