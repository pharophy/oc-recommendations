## Description

The Lost Inferno is a South County pirate-tiki hideout built around Goonies energy, rum-forward drinks, and a tongue-in-cheek adventure-bar identity instead of polished luxury tropical service.

## What Makes It Unique

It is one of the clearest "themed bar first, normal hospitality second" picks in Orange County. The pirate framing, secret-club voice, and recurring magic-show programming make it feel much more specific than a generic tiki room.

## Notes

- Reservations: The official site offers reservations and a waitlist.
- Dress code: Casual.
- Age policy: The official homepage currently says `Open for Cocktails (21 & Over For Now, Food Soon)`, so I marked it `No` for kids.
- Other: The same address also hosts `Momoku No Usagi`, which works as a second distinct stop if you want a more intimate speakeasy after this one.
