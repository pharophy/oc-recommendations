## Description

Stowaway is one of Orange County's strongest all-around tiki rooms: a full restaurant-bar build with dense escapist decor, flaming drinks, rum depth, and enough food to work as an actual night out rather than just a cocktail stop.

## What Makes It Unique

It commits to transportive set design instead of a light beach-bar theme. The room is built as a midcentury tropical fantasy with collected artifacts, layered lighting, theatrical mug service, and enough atmosphere to feel like its own destination.
