## Description

The Gallery's `Elementa` program is one of the clearest SoCal examples of an immersive dinner show built around a full five-course progression, projection mapping, scenic world-building, and theatrical hosting.

## What Makes It Unique

This is not just "dinner with decor." The dining room is designed as a programmable environment where visuals, music, and table interactions shift course by course. It reads more like a theme-park-caliber hospitality production than a standard restaurant activation.
