## Description

Royal Hawaiian is a revived Laguna Beach classic that leans into historic tiki-bar status, coastal location, and full-room Polynesian decor rather than novelty alone.

## What Makes It Unique

The draw is the combination of age, atmosphere, and setting. It reads like a proper Orange County destination because it is both a historic room and a lively beach-town escape, not just another modern tiki imitation.

## Notes

- Reservations: The official site and OpenTable both support reservations.
- Dress code: OpenTable lists `Resort Casual`.
- Age policy: Royal Hawaiian's official blog FAQ and OpenTable both frame it as family-friendly, so I marked it `Yes`.
- Other: Better for a broad crowd than some of the stricter speakeasy picks; also one of the easier immersive spots to recommend to visitors staying near the coast.
