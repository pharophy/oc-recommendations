## Description

The Queen Mary's Royal Sunday Brunch turns a buffet into a historic-transport fantasy, staged in the ship's former first-class Grand Salon with live music, Art Deco atmosphere, and a broad special-occasion spread.

## What Makes It Unique

This is one of the clearest Southern California answers to "travel nostalgia as dining." Like Pan Am, the appeal comes from inhabiting a preserved glamour-era vehicle of travel, not just eating in a pretty room.

## Notes

- Reservations: The official brunch page says reservations are required and highlights online booking.
- Dress code: I did not find a strict dress code on the brunch page in this pass, but the setting skews dressier than a normal buffet.
- Age policy: Suitable for families and multigenerational groups.
- Other: The official page currently advertises more than 50 dishes, live entertainment, and service on Sundays from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. in the Grand Salon.
