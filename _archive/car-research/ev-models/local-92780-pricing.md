# Local Inventory And Estimated OTD Pricing

ZIP used: `92780`  
Research date: `March 26, 2026`

## Method

- I searched current local inventory pages and listing results around `Tustin / Orange County / nearby 30 miles`.
- Dealers almost never publish true out-the-door pricing online.
- For comparability, I estimated `OTD` as:

`advertised price + 9.25% sales tax + $85 doc fee + about $700 CA title/registration/plate estimate`

That is a working estimate, not a binding quote. Actual OTD can move based on:

- dealer add-ons
- accessory packages
- DMV / VLF variance
- financing-contingent discounts
- conditional incentives you may not qualify for
