# Crawford Canyon Park

Crawford Canyon Park is a newer OC Parks destination that stands out because it combines two nature-themed playgrounds with a paved walking loop, picnic areas, and enough supporting infrastructure to make it feel built for lingering.

## Why It Stands Out

Many playgrounds are good only for a short stop. Crawford Canyon Park is stronger because it feels like a complete family park experience, with newer design, more room to move, and a setting that supports longer visits.

## Practical Notes

- OC Parks opened the park in late 2025.
- The site includes two nature-themed playgrounds.
- There is a quarter-mile paved walkway.
- Picnic tables and benches are available throughout the park.
- Daily hours vary by season.
- The park includes ADA parking stalls and sidewalk access.
