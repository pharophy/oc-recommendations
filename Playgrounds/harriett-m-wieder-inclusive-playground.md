# Harriett M. Wieder Inclusive Playground

Harriett M. Wieder Regional Park now has one of the strongest accessibility-focused playgrounds in Orange County, with a redesign that makes the park notable not just for views and open space but for inclusive play itself.

## Why It Stands Out

This is a destination-level playground because the design intent is clear and substantial. It is not simply a standard playground with a token accessible element added afterward.

## Practical Notes

- OC Parks reopened the ADA-accessible playground in February 2025.
- The site includes separate play areas for ages 2 to 5 and ages 5 to 12.
- Features include sensory panels and a glider designed for mobility-device access.
- Park hours are 7:00 a.m. to sunset.
- OC Parks identifies it as a PlayCore National Demonstration Site.
