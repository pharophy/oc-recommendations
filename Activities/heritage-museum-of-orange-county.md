# Heritage Museum of Orange County

Heritage Museum of Orange County is a useful anchor for local culture and living-history outings. The site combines historic buildings, gardens, and event programming in a way that feels more experiential than a typical indoor museum.

## Why It Stands Out

This place works especially well if you like events with local character. Even outside big festivals, the grounds themselves support a slower, more atmospheric visit than a quick exhibit stop.

## Practical Notes

- HMOC hosts open houses, seasonal events, teas, living-history programming, and community celebrations.
- The site is also one of the better local anchors for Dia de los Muertos and other heritage-focused programming.
- Check the museum calendar because access is more event-driven than a large daily-admission museum.
