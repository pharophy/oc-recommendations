# Spotlight OC at Mercado Gonzalez

Spotlight OC is a new UCI Continuing Education series built around guided cultural excursions. One of its strongest early entries is the Mercado Gonzalez session in Costa Mesa, which turns a visit to the market into a hosted food-and-culture experience rather than a casual drop-in.

## Why It Stands Out

Mercado Gonzalez already feels immersive on its own. The Spotlight OC format makes it more distinctive by adding context, structure, and a hosted itinerary. That gives it more value than a typical self-guided market visit, especially for a date or group outing.

## Practical Notes

- UCI announced Spotlight OC in March 2026.
- The Mercado Gonzalez session is listed for April 18, 2026 from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
- The listed fee is $75.
- This is positioned more for adults than for a young-kids outing.
