# Mission San Juan Capistrano

Mission San Juan Capistrano is one of Orange County's clearest "unique to this area" destinations. It works as both a beautiful outing and a child-friendly learning experience because the grounds, history, gardens, and hands-on activities all reinforce each other.

## Why It Stands Out

This is not just a historic building. It is a full place-based experience tied to California and Orange County history, with enough atmosphere and hands-on elements to feel memorable for both adults and kids.

## Practical Notes

- The Mission is open Tuesday through Sunday from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
- Official daily activities include koi feeding, the Mission Clubhouse, sticker tours, scavenger hunts, and audio tours.
- The Mission Clubhouse includes basket weaving, mission job activities, and miniature Roman arch building.
- Grounds are historic and uneven, so comfortable shoes help.
