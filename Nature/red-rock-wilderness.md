# Red Rock Wilderness

Red Rock Wilderness is one of the most visually unusual landscapes in Orange County, with rust-colored rock formations, exposed ridges, and overlook views that feel very different from the region's more common suburban-adjacent trails.

## Why It Stands Out

The terrain gives this area a much stronger visual identity than most local hikes. It is one of the best choices when you want a trail that looks distinct in photos and feels closer to a mini escape than a routine exercise walk.

## Practical Notes

- The wilderness area covers roughly 1,500 acres with more than 8 miles of trails.
- A major draw is the 360-degree overlook.
- Access is generally managed through registration-based programs and events.
- Parking is free.
- Dogs are not permitted.
