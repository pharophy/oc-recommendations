# Dripping Cave at Aliso and Wood Canyons

Dripping Cave is one of the strongest "this feels different" hikes in South Orange County. The cave itself gives the route a real destination, and the surrounding canyon landscape adds a more hidden, exploratory feel.

## Why It Stands Out

If part of the appeal of Black Star Canyon Falls was that the hike felt like an outing with a memorable payoff, this is one of the best next moves. It has a stronger sense of place than a generic overlook or exercise trail.

## Practical Notes

- The cave sits inside Aliso and Wood Canyons Wilderness Park.
- Terrain can be uneven, and the route is better for families comfortable with hiking than for stroller outings.
- Cooler mornings are the best fit.
- Check park conditions before visiting.
