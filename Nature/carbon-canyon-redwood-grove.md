# Carbon Canyon Redwood Grove

Carbon Canyon Regional Park has one of the most surprising nature features in the county: a grove of coastal redwoods tucked into inland Orange County. That contrast alone makes it more memorable than a standard park loop.

## Why It Stands Out

Seeing redwoods in Orange County feels genuinely odd in the best way. The grove gives the park a destination-quality hook that works for families, casual walkers, and anyone looking for an easy nature outing with a novelty factor.

## Practical Notes

- The grove is reached by a roughly 1.1-mile nature trail.
- OC Parks describes it as Orange County's only grove of coastal redwoods.
- Park entry fees apply.
- This works well as a low-effort walk rather than a full hiking day.
