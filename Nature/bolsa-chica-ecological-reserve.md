# Bolsa Chica Ecological Reserve

Bolsa Chica is one of the county's biggest and most atmospheric wetland landscapes, with open sky, long sightlines, bird habitat, and easy walking paths that make it feel very different from the typical coastal bike-path experience.

## Why It Stands Out

This is one of the strongest easy-access birding and wetlands outings in Orange County. It works well when you want scenery and wildlife without a strenuous hike or complicated logistics.

## Practical Notes

- The reserve spans about 1,400 acres.
- Visitors can explore roughly 5 miles of trails.
- The reserve is open sunrise to sunset.
- Parking is free.
- Dogs are not allowed.
